According to Payscale's 2021 Compensation Best Practices Report, the most common types of compensation packages offered by employers include base salary, bonuses, and benefits such as health insurance, retirement plans, and paid time off. The report also found that the most common factors that influence compensation decisions include job performance, market competition, and employee retention.

In terms of industry standards and market trends, Payscale's data shows that the highest-paying industries for professionals with similar skills and experience include technology, finance, and healthcare. Within these industries, job titles such as software engineer, data scientist, financial analyst, and physician are among the highest-paying.

Overall, it's important to note that compensation packages can vary widely depending on the specific job title, industry, and location. It's always a good idea to research and compare compensation data for your specific situation to ensure that you are being fairly compensated.
